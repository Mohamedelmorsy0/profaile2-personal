Motivation to continue

Sometimes traders in the financial markets feel a state of fear and anxiety, and the trader may then make wrong decisions and this may increase his loss. But it's best not to panic or despair when you have that condition. It is better to look from time to time for motivational statements from pioneers of trading in the financial markets, as this is one of the biggest motivations that help you control your nerves. Among the most prominent sayings of the most famous traders:

1// You can't get money by just trading. (Jesse Livermore)

2// If we don't take into account the risks, they will eliminate us. (Larry Hight)

3// Allowing loss to spread is one of the biggest mistakes traders make. (William O'Nial)

4// You have to be prepared to make mistakes in order to learn and make more profits. (Bruce Kofner)

5// I am not happy when the deal reaches the target, because it is normal to save my money from losing. (Marty Schwartz)

6// One of the biggest reasons we lose money when trading in the financial markets is the inability to limit our losses. (Victor Sprando)
